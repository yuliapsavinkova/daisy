Common questions

I have multiple dogs — is the price still $30 a day?
Yes, the more the merrier! As long as your dogs can be walked together, there's no extra charge. If they need to be walked separately — temperament, size, or training — that counts as additional overhead and we'll factor it into the rate.
Do you sit in any home?
I ask that the home is clean, comfortable, and well-maintained. I put a lot of care into every sit — I simply ask for the same in return. If you're proud of your home, we'll get along great.
Do you have references or background checks?
Yes — available upon request.
How do we confirm a booking?
We schedule a video call, confirm expectations, then a written agreement.
What happens in an emergency?
I contact you immediately and follow your vet/emergency plan.
Why the $30 charge if I can get you for free on THS?
On THS, I'm a traveler exploring a new place — technically I select you, not the other way around. When you book me directly, I'm there strictly for you, not for my travel agenda.
