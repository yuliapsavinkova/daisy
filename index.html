Who I am
I'm Yulia. I run my own web development company remotely, which means I can work from anywhere, so I do. House-sitting lets me stay in real homes instead of hotels in exchange for looking after the property, the plants, and the furry residents.

Experience & trust
I grew up with pets and genuinely enjoy spending time with them. Since this travel lifestyle doesn’t allow me to have my own, each sit is a welcome chance to have that connection again. I’ve been house-sitting full time for over a year, so most situations that come up are things I’ve likely handled before. Earlier in my career, I spent 10 years in the tech industry, and I bring that same reliability and work ethic to every sit.

Why it's affordable
Because house-sitting is how I travel, not how I make a living, I can offer this for a fraction of what a boarding kennel or traditional sitter would charge without compromising on care. The only thing I ask for in return is a clean, comfortable, and well-maintained home to work from.

Common questions

Why the $30 charge if I can book you through TrustedHousesitters?
I encourage you to invite me through TrustedHousesitters and see if I am available in your area for your dates. You must be a TrustedHousesitters member and pay the platform fee. For direct bookings, the $30/day also reflects that I'm traveling specifically to you — rather than a sit that's already on my route. It also helps offset the protections I give up: through TrustedHousesitters I'm covered by accident & liability insurance, a sit cancellation plan, and access to emergency vet support.
Do you sit in any home?
Since I live in your home, the condition matters — not just for comfort, but for my ability to work remotely. I expect a clean, tidy space, working essentials (reliable wifi, hot water, heating/cooling), and a home that's maintained rather than handed over with a list of things that are broken. I treat your home with care and ask for the same standard in return.
Why live in full time instead of drop-in visits?
Routine and presence. Staying in the home keeps your pets' schedule consistent and avoids the stress that comes from long gaps alone. If you're looking for drop-in visits or dog walking, Rover is the best platform for that — you can find me there too.
I have multiple dogs — is the price still $30 a day?
Yes. If they get along and can be walked together, the rate remains $30 per day. If they require separate handling, we can discuss a custom plan to accommodate their needs.
How do we confirm a booking?
We schedule a video call, agree on expectations, and confirm with a written agreement.
Do you have references or background checks?
Yes. References and background verification are available upon request.
What happens in an emergency?
I contact you immediately and follow your provided veterinary or emergency instructions.
