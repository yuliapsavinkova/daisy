Common questions

Why live in full time instead of drop-in visits?
Routine and presence. Staying in the home keeps your pets' schedule consistent and avoids the stress that comes from long gaps alone.
Do you sit in any home?
The home should be clean, comfortable, and well-maintained. I care for your home as I would my own, and expect the same standard.
I have multiple dogs — is the price still $30 a day?
Yes. If they can be walked together, the rate remains $30 per day. If they require separate walks due to size, temperament, or training, we'll adjust the rate accordingly.
Why the $30 charge if I can book you through TrustedHousesitters?
I encourage you to reach out to check availability in your area for $0; you must be a THS member and pay the platform fee.
How do we confirm a booking?
We schedule a video call, agree on expectations, and confirm with a written agreement.
Do you have references or background checks?
Yes. References and background verification are available upon request.
What happens in an emergency?
I contact you immediately and follow your provided veterinary or emergency instructions.
